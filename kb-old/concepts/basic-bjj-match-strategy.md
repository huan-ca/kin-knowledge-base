# Basic BJJ Match Strategy

## Definition
Students should be taught the logic of a BJJ match, not only isolated techniques.

## Key Details
- Top-game strategy moves from stance to grip fighting, takedown, lower-body control, pass, upper-body control, position advancement, and submission.
- A parallel guard strategy includes grip fight, guard pull, posture breaking, sweep, and submission.
- Positional hierarchy and leverage should be easier for students to understand than isolated move lists.

## Gaps
- The source emphasizes strategic understanding, but individual tournament strategies still need event-specific adaptation.
