# Turtle System

## Definition
Turtle is treated as a dynamic defensive shell where the top player seeks breakdowns or back exposure and the bottom player seeks recovery or stand-up opportunities.

## Key Details
- Top control uses chest pressure, seatbelt or power-half structure, tight knee placement, and active weight.
- Top attacks include clock choke, rear naked choke, armbar, kimura, and crucifix.
- Major transitions include back takes, truck entries, and breakdowns to side control.

## Gaps
- The source does not fully separate youth-safe turtle attacks from advanced adult-only variations.
