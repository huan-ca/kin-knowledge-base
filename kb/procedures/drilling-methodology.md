# Drilling Methodology

## Definition
Drilling is framed as the bridge between technique demonstration and open sparring, and it should be specific, progressive, and goal-oriented.

## Key Details
- Solo drills support movement pattern development, especially in warm ups.
- Call-out drills cue specific reactions from defined positions.
- Pocket drills restrict the exchange to one tactical area.
- Objective drills assign measurable goals such as a submission or score.
- Reset drills restart after the target outcome is reached.
- Progressive overload is used so students can raise speed and resistance over time.
