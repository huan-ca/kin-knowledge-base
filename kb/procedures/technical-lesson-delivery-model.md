# Technical Lesson Delivery Model

## Definition
New technique instruction should follow a short, repeatable sequence that prioritizes clarity and controlled repetition over long explanations.

## Key Details
- Demonstrate in real time first.
- Break the movement down slowly while emphasizing main mechanics.
- Limit full demonstrations to roughly three repetitions.
- Let students attempt the technique early.
- Bring students back to correct common mistakes.
- Continue drilling under minimal then progressive resistance.

## Gaps
- The source does not define separate delivery variants for every age or belt cohort.
