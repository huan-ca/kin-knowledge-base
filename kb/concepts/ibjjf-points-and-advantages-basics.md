# IBJJF Points and Advantages Basics

## Definition
The curriculum uses the IBJJF point system as a strategic teaching baseline because many events follow that ruleset.

## Detailed Notes
- Takedowns and knee ride score 2 points.
- Guard pass scores 3 points.
- Mount and rear mount score 4 points.
- Positions generally need to be stabilized for roughly 3 seconds to score.
- Advantages are tie-breaker tools for near-scoring actions when regular points are absent.

## Gaps
- The source gives a practical summary, not a full competition rulebook.
