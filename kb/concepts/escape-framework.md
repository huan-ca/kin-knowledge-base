# Escape Framework

## Definition
Escapes are treated as the most important confidence-building layer for kids and are organized around survival, structure, recovery, and reversal.

## Key Details
- Mount, side control, rear mount, knee on belly, north-south, turtle, and half guard each have dedicated escape logic.
- The emphasis is on frames, posture, re-guarding, and controlled reversals.
- Escapes are explicitly positioned as a prerequisite for productive guard and submission training.

## Gaps
- The source contains many escape details, but not a finalized youth progression for which escapes are taught first.
