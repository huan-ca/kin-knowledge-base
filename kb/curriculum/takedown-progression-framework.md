# Takedown Progression Framework

## Definition
Takedown instruction is organized as a two-level progression that should feed directly into weekly ground themes.

## Key Details
- Level 1 is a basic takedown.
- Level 2 is an advanced option, variation, or chain from a similar takedown family.
- The landing position should align with the week’s ground work.

## Gaps
- The framework does not itself name the full sequence of takedowns for the full 24 weeks.
