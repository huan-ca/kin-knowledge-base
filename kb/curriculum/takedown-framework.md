# Takedown Framework

## Definition
The takedown curriculum spans judo, freestyle wrestling, and Greco-style clinch work while still preserving level 1 and level 2 progression logic.

## Key Details
- Judo families include foot sweeps, hip throws, hand throws, and sacrifice throws.
- Wrestling families include picks, snaps, shots, and drags.
- Greco-style content includes clinch work, rear takedowns, collar ties, duck unders, throw-bys, and Russian ties.
- Curriculum logic expects throws and takedowns to land into the week’s ground theme.

## Gaps
- The framework names many families, but a final youth scope still needs to prioritize which ones appear first.
