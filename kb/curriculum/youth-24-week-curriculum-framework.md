# Youth 24-Week Curriculum Framework

## Definition
The long-form youth curriculum is expected to run for 24 weeks and to follow the Kin BJJ compendium while preserving a weekly match-like theme.

## Key Details
- Every week should include takedowns, ground work, and submissions.
- The curriculum should alternate between offensive and defensive cycles every two weeks.
- Level 1 and level 2 options should be present for each area.
- Weekly content should connect like real exchanges in matches.

## Gaps
- The source gives design constraints but not the final week-by-week calendar.
