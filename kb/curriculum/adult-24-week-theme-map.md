# Adult 24-Week Theme Map

## Definition
This page mirrors the youth positional and thematic sequence for adult classes while filtering the content through adult-specific pacing, rule-set awareness, and broader lower-body submission preparation in the final two weeks.

## Sequencing Notes
- Weeks 1 to 22 intentionally align with the youth sequence so the academy can maintain a shared positional language.
- Adult files should still stay separate so coaches only see the context that matters to their room.
- Weeks 23 and 24 broaden into lower-body submission work with explicit safety framing and awareness of IBJJF plus non-IBJJF environments.
