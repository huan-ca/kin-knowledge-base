# Youth 24-Week Theme Map

## Definition
This page proposes the canonical youth week-by-week curriculum sequence for ages 8 to 13. It converts the existing KIN framework into a fixed 24-week thematic map while keeping coaching delivery flexible inside each week.

## Sequencing Notes
- Weeks 1 to 22 stay inside the survival, escape, guard, passing, pin, submission, and takedown progression implied by the KIN material.
- Offensive and defensive cycles stay grouped in two-week blocks where practical.
- Survival details remain embedded inside escape-focused weeks rather than becoming a standalone module.
- Weeks 23 and 24 reserve youth-specific self-defense themes for standing and ground contexts.
