# Curriculum Week Design Rules

## Definition
Weekly curriculum design follows a fixed set of rules intended to keep theme, progression, and coach usability aligned.

## Key Details
- Each week should contain takedowns, ground work, and submissions.
- Level 2 should add complexity, transitions, or chains rather than just duplication.
- Each section should include coach-useful teaching details.
- Themes should connect across the whole week as they would in a match.
- Offensive and defensive cycles should stay grouped where possible.

## Gaps
- The rules guide sequencing but do not yet define a finished weekly implementation.
