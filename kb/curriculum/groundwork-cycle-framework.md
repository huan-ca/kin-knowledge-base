# Groundwork Cycle Framework

## Definition
Groundwork is structured around survival, staged escapes and reversals, guard cycles, passing styles, and pin or transition categories.

## Key Details
- Survival is included within escape teaching rather than given its own week.
- Escapes and reversals should be taught in early, middle, and late stages.
- Guard work includes both offensive and defensive cycles.
- Passing styles include pressure, movement, and submission-based approaches.
- Pin themes can be grouped by secure or control, attack or submit, and advance or transition.

## Gaps
- The source does not fully map which positional family appears in which week.
