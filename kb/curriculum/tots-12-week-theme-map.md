# Tots 12-Week Theme Map

## Definition
This page defines a 12-week tots sequence for ages 4 to 7. It is intentionally movement-led, game-heavy, and lighter on technical density than the youth and adult curriculum.

## Sequencing Notes
- Every week should feel playful and athletic first, technical second.
- Simple grappling exposure is included, but the main goals are movement confidence, coordination, attention, and comfort on the mat.
- Coaches should adapt pacing and partner work to the maturity and energy of the room.
