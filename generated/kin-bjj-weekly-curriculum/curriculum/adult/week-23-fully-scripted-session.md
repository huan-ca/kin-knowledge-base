# Adult Week 23 Fully Scripted Session

## Metadata
- Week: 23
- Cycle: offensive
- Theme: Lower-Body Offense: Entries, Ashi Position, And Controlled Finishes
- Teaching Goal: Introduce lower-body submission offense through safe entries, control, and pressure management.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Lower-Body Offense: Entries, Ashi Position, And Controlled Finishes. Tell the class the goal is Introduce lower-body submission offense through safe entries, control, and pressure management.

## Warm-Up Block
Use a brief, topic-aware warm-up and set the expectation that the class will build one connected theme.

## Primary Focus Script
### Lesson: Safe leg-entanglement entries, ashi positioning, and controlled straight-footlock style finishing mechanics

- **Ground**
  - Coaching tip: "Control the knee line and partner safety before adding finishing pressure"
  - Level 1: Enter one stable lower-body control and maintain it safely

- **Submission: Footlock**
  - Level 2: Chain the first entry to a second control or follow-up finish when the defender rotates
  - Outcome: Introduce lower-body submission offense through safe entries, control, and pressure management

## Adult-Specific Notes
Mention IBJJF legality, but explain that the academy wants students aware of broader rule sets and defensive realities.

## Closing Script
Recap the main idea, reinforce the best example from the room, and set the expectation for live application.
