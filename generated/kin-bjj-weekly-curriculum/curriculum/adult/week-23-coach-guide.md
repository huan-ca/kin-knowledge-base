# Adult Week 23 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 23
- Cycle: offensive
- Theme: Lower-Body Offense: Entries, Ashi Position, And Controlled Finishes
- Teaching Goal: Introduce lower-body submission offense through safe entries, control, and pressure management.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Introduce lower-body submission offense through safe entries, control, and pressure management.

## Coaching Emphasis
Frame the week as positional control first, finishing second.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Mention IBJJF legality, but explain that the academy wants students aware of broader rule sets and defensive realities.
