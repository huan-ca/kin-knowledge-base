# Adult Week 12 Fully Scripted Session

## Metadata
- Week: 12
- Cycle: offensive
- Theme: Mount Progression To Armbar And Collar Attack
- Teaching Goal: Connect mount climbing, isolation, and attack selection under realistic defensive reactions.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Mount Progression To Armbar And Collar Attack. Tell the class the goal is Connect mount climbing, isolation, and attack selection under realistic defensive reactions.

## Warm-Up Block
Use a brief, topic-aware warm-up and set the expectation that the class will build one connected theme.

## Primary Focus Script
### Lesson: Mount control, high mount progression, and armbar or collar-based attack entries

- **Ground: Mount**
  - Coaching tip: "The mount should feel heavy and balanced before any attack starts"
  - Level 1: Climb to a stable attacking mount and finish one clean armbar pathway

- **Submission: Armbar**
  - Level 2: Switch between armbar and collar attack based on the defender’s hands
  - Outcome: Connect mount climbing, isolation, and attack selection under realistic defensive reactions

## Adult-Specific Notes
Discuss how mount attacks differ across gi and no-gi contexts while keeping the main chain stable.

## Closing Script
Recap the main idea, reinforce the best example from the room, and set the expectation for live application.
