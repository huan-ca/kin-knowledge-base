## Coach Notes
Teach the room to read pressure changes instead of memorizing one exact motion.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Use alternating mount and knee-on-belly pressure so the bottom player must connect escapes dynamically.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Defensive reactions from mount and knee on belly into half guard or full guard recovery.
- Coach Notes: Escapes should preserve elbows and neck position before anything else.

## Level 1
- Bridge, frame, and recover a safe half guard.

## Level 2
- Chain bridge and elbow-knee reactions when the top player floats between mount and knee on belly.
