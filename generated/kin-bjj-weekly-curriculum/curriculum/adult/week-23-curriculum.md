## Coach Notes
Frame the week as positional control first, finishing second.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Mention IBJJF legality, but explain that the academy wants students aware of broader rule sets and defensive realities.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Safe leg-entanglement entries, ashi positioning, and controlled straight-footlock style finishing mechanics.
- Coach Notes: Control the knee line and partner safety before adding finishing pressure.

## Level 1
- Enter one stable lower-body control and maintain it safely.

## Level 2
- Chain the first entry to a second control or follow-up finish when the defender rotates.
