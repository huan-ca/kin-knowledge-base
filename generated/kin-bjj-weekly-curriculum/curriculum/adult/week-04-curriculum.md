## Adult-Specific Notes
Mention the IBJJF safety lens, but also explain how the same mechanics show up across broader rule sets.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Closed guard armbar and triangle pathway with controlled finishing details.
- Coach Notes: Frame the chain as posture break, angle, isolate, finish.

## Level 1
- Attack the straight armbar or triangle after clearing posture.

## Level 2
- Chain armbar to triangle or back to posture-breaking if the first attack stalls.
