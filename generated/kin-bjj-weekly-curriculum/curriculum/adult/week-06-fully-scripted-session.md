# Adult Week 06 Fully Scripted Session

## Metadata
- Week: 06
- Cycle: defensive
- Theme: Seated And De La Riva Recovery Reactions
- Teaching Goal: Use seated and De La Riva reactions to restore usable guard structure after grips break.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Seated And De La Riva Recovery Reactions. Tell the class the goal is Use seated and De La Riva reactions to restore usable guard structure after grips break.

## Warm-Up Block
Use a brief, topic-aware warm-up and set the expectation that the class will build one connected theme.

## Primary Focus Script
### Lesson: Recovering seated guard and De La Riva hooks after the passer clears the first line

- **Primary Focus**
  - Coaching tip: "Adults should feel how hook placement changes the passer’s posture"
  - Level 1: Rebuild frames and reconnect a seated or De La Riva entry
  - Level 2: Use a second hook, collar grip, or angle change to off-balance the passer before recovering
  - Outcome: Use seated and De La Riva reactions to restore usable guard structure after grips break

- **CLA/Situational Rolling Options**:
  - Start from the core position in Recovering seated guard and De La Riva hooks after the passer clears the first line and work until rebuild frames and reconnect a seated or de la riva entry or the position resets.
  - Start from a successful first exchange and continue until use a second hook, collar grip, or angle change to off-balance the passer before recovering or the defender escapes.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Keep the room grounded in guard recovery and off-balance basics before discussing more advanced inversions.

## Closing Script
Recap the main idea, reinforce the best example from the room, and set the expectation for live application.
