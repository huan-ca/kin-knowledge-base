# Tots Week 06 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 06
- Cycle: movement
- Theme: Balance, Posture, And Push-Pull Games
- Movement Theme: Staying tall while resisting light pushes and pulls.
- Class Length: 30 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Balance, Posture, And Push-Pull Games

## Coaching Emphasis
Use playful resistance and quick partner switches.

## Safety Notes
Keep rounds short, maintain high engagement, and prioritize movement quality over technical volume.

## Scaling Options
- Reduce complexity by shrinking the movement chain.
- Increase challenge by adding reaction and direction changes.
