# Tots Week 12 Fully Scripted Session

## Metadata
- Week: 12
- Cycle: review
- Theme: Review Circuit And Fun Challenge Day
- Movement Theme: Mixing the favorite movements from the cycle into one class.
- Class Length: 30 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Review Circuit And Fun Challenge Day. Tell the class the goal is Review Circuit And Fun Challenge Day.

## Warm-Up Block
Run a movement-focused warm-up around Mixing the favorite movements from the cycle into one class. and keep the pace playful.

## Main Activity Block
Play Obstacle course review day with stations. and use short resets to reinforce Confidence, transitions, and listening through changing tasks..

## Intro Grappling Block
Expose the students to Review of stance, falls, bridges, shrimping, stand-up, and partner movement. with simple cues and lots of praise.

## Closing Script
Recap the main idea, reinforce the best example from the room, and set the expectation for live application.
