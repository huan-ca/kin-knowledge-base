# Youth Week 14 Fully Scripted Session

## Metadata
- Week: 14
- Cycle: defensive
- Theme: Back Survival, Hand Fighting, And Rear-Escape Chains
- Teaching Goal: Give students a structured method for surviving the back and removing hooks or choking control.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Back Survival, Hand Fighting, And Rear-Escape Chains. Tell the class the goal is Give students a structured method for surviving the back and removing hooks or choking control.

## Warm-Up Block
Use a brief, topic-aware warm-up and set the expectation that the class will build one connected theme.

## Primary Focus Script
### Lesson: Back defense, two-on-one hand fighting, and escaping to safe top or guard positions

- **Primary Focus**
  - Coaching tip: "The first win is surviving the neck, not escaping fast"
  - Level 1: Clear the choking threat and escape one hook line
  - Level 2: Chain the first escape into a turn or top-position recovery before the attacker resets
  - Outcome: Give students a structured method for surviving the back and removing hooks or choking control

## Closing Script
Recap the main idea, reinforce the best example from the room, and set the expectation for live application.
