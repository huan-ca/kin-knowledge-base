# Youth Week 12 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 12
- Cycle: offensive
- Theme: Mount Progression To Armbar And Collar Attack
- Teaching Goal: Build mount progression from control into one clear attack pathway and one transition option.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Build mount progression from control into one clear attack pathway and one transition option.

## Coaching Emphasis
Students should understand when to stay mounted and when to advance the attack.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.
