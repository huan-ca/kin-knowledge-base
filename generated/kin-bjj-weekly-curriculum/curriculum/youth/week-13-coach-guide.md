# Youth Week 13 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 13
- Cycle: defensive
- Theme: Turtle Posture, Sit-Outs, And Front-Headlock Defense
- Teaching Goal: Use turtle as a dynamic defensive station instead of a hiding position.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Use turtle as a dynamic defensive station instead of a hiding position.

## Coaching Emphasis
Students should learn when to stay compact and when to move to their feet or guard.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.
