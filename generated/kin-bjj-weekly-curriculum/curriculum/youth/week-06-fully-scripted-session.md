# Youth Week 06 Fully Scripted Session

## Metadata
- Week: 06
- Cycle: defensive
- Theme: Seated And De La Riva Recovery Reactions
- Teaching Goal: Build defensive reactions from seated and De La Riva style guard positions when grips or hooks break down.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Seated And De La Riva Recovery Reactions. Tell the class the goal is Build defensive reactions from seated and De La Riva style guard positions when grips or hooks break down.

## Warm-Up Block
Use a brief, topic-aware warm-up and set the expectation that the class will build one connected theme.

## Primary Focus Script
### Lesson: Recovering seated guard and De La Riva hooks after the passer clears the first line

- **Primary Focus**
  - Coaching tip: "Teach students to win the inside space back before looking for offense"
  - Level 1: Rebuild frames and reconnect a seated or De La Riva entry
  - Level 2: Use a second hook, collar grip, or angle change to off-balance the passer before recovering
  - Outcome: Build defensive reactions from seated and De La Riva style guard positions when grips or hooks break down

## Closing Script
Recap the main idea, reinforce the best example from the room, and set the expectation for live application.
