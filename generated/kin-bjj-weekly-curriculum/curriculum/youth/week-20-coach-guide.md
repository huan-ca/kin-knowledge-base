# Youth Week 20 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 20
- Cycle: offensive
- Theme: Double Leg And Body-Lock Entries To Passing Finish
- Teaching Goal: Blend wrestling-style takedown entries with immediate passing and settling behavior on the ground.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Blend wrestling-style takedown entries with immediate passing and settling behavior on the ground.

## Coaching Emphasis
The takedown is only the first half of the week; the finish is the real measure.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.
