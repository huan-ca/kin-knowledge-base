## Coach Notes
Adults often rush to submissions; slow them down enough to win posture first.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Use posture resistance and realistic hand-fighting to make the sweep entries honest.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Closed guard posture breaking and hip-bump style sweep threats.
- Coach Notes: Strong angle and posture control matter more than trying to muscle the sweep.

## Level 1
- Break posture and connect a hip-bump sweep or sit-up sweep entry.

## Level 2
- Use the sweep threat to force a post and connect to a second attack.
