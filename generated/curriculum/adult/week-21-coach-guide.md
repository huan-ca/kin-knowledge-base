# Adult Week 21 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 21
- Cycle: defensive
- Theme: Armbar And Triangle Defense
- Teaching Goal: Use posture and angle awareness to survive and unwind common upper-body submission threats.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Use posture and angle awareness to survive and unwind common upper-body submission threats.

## Coaching Emphasis
Students need a clean order of operations more than a lot of exotic escapes.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Tie the defense to safe passing or reset behavior rather than leaving the exchange neutral.
