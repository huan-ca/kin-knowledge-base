## Coach Notes
A good takedown here is one that lands in a useful control, not just one that looks sharp.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Adults can handle more hand-fighting and setup detail, especially around kuzushi and head position.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Inside trips and outer reaps that land directly into side control or scarf-hold pressure.
- Coach Notes: Finish the throw and settle immediately.

## Level 1
- Hit one clean takedown and settle into top control before the partner scrambles.

## Level 2
- Chain the first off-balance into a second re-attack when the partner steps out.
