## Coach Notes
Encourage students to solve pressure with timing, not only grit.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Allow the top player to float between mount and knee-on-belly so the bottom player must read transitions.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Middle and late-stage escape chains from mount, knee on belly, and heavy side control.
- Coach Notes: Late escapes should still end in a structurally sound guard, not a scramble.

## Level 1
- Use elbow-knee movement and hip recovery to rebuild guard.

## Level 2
- Add an ankle scoop or turn-in reaction when the top player overcommits.
