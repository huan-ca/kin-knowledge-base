# Adult Week 17 Coach Guide

## Weekly Intent
Use standing defense to build confidence against pressure and takedown attempts.

## Coaching Emphasis
Adults should feel the relationship between stance, head position, and safe reactions.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Bring in more hand-fighting detail and posture breaking than in the youth version.
