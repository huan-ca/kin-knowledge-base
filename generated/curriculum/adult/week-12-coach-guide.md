# Adult Week 12 Coach Guide

## Weekly Intent
Connect mount climbing, isolation, and attack selection under realistic defensive reactions.

## Coaching Emphasis
Force the class to win the mount before letting them chase the finish.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Discuss how mount attacks differ across gi and no-gi contexts while keeping the main chain stable.
