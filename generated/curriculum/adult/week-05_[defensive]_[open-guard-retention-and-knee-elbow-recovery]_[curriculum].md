## Coach Notes
Make the class competitive through reaction games and constraint rounds.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Use mixed passing styles so students must recover against both pressure and angle.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Open guard retention, hip turns, and knee-elbow recovery against passing pressure.
- Coach Notes: Feet, knees, and frames should reconnect before the passer settles hips.

## Level 1
- Recover one knee line and rebuild a square guard.

## Level 2
- Add a second leg pummel or angle change to recover a stronger guard family.
