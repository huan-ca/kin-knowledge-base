# Adult Week 07 Coach Guide

## Weekly Intent
Connect headquarters structure to a controlled passing lane that finishes with chest pressure.

## Coaching Emphasis
Do not let the class mistake movement for finished passing.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Build some rounds from headquarters with progressive resistance before allowing full passing.
