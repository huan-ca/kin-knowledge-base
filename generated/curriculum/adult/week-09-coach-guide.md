# Adult Week 09 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 09
- Cycle: defensive
- Theme: Side Control Escape Layers And Kesa Survival
- Teaching Goal: Handle side control pressure and scarf-hold variants without overexposing the back or arms.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Handle side control pressure and scarf-hold variants without overexposing the back or arms.

## Coaching Emphasis
Keep this week brutally practical and pressure-tested.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Allow more weight and chest connection from the top player than in youth classes.
