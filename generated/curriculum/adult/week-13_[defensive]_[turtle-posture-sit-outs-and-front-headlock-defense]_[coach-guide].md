# Adult Week 13 Coach Guide

## Weekly Intent
Use turtle defensively without letting it become a dead-end shell.

## Coaching Emphasis
Students should learn when to stay compact and when to move immediately.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Give adults more freedom to wrestle up or build front-headlock counters once the basic defensive structure is stable.
