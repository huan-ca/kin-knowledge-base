# Adult Week 05 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 05
- Cycle: defensive
- Theme: Open Guard Retention And Knee-Elbow Recovery
- Teaching Goal: Sharpen retention and recovery mechanics against pressure and movement passing.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Sharpen retention and recovery mechanics against pressure and movement passing.

## Coaching Emphasis
Make the class competitive through reaction games and constraint rounds.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Use mixed passing styles so students must recover against both pressure and angle.
