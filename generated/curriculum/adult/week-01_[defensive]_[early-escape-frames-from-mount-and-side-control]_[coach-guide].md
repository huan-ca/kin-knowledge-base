# Adult Week 01 Coach Guide

## Weekly Intent
Reinforce calm survival habits and first-stage escapes from the most common major pins.

## Coaching Emphasis
Adults can handle faster resistance increases, but structure still comes before urgency.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Use heavier pressure and more realistic posting reactions than the youth room once mechanics are established.
