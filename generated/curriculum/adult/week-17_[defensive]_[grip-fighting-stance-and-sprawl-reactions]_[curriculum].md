## Adult-Specific Notes
Bring in more hand-fighting detail and posture breaking than in the youth version.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Grip fighting, stance discipline, level-change awareness, and sprawls.
- Coach Notes: The stance should recover immediately after each defense.

## Level 1
- Win inside position and sprawl safely against a basic shot.

## Level 2
