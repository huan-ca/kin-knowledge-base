# Adult Week 15 Coach Guide

## Weekly Intent
Turn rear control into a high-percentage finishing system built on patient control and clean mechanics.

## Coaching Emphasis
If the class loses hooks and seatbelt control, they are not ready to finish yet.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.

## Adult-Specific Notes
Use realistic hand-fighting reactions before releasing students into live finishing rounds.
