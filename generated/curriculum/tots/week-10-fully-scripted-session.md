# Tots Week 10 Fully Scripted Session

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Escape Movement And Space-Making. Tell the class the goal is Escape Movement And Space-Making.

## Warm-Up Block
Run a movement-focused warm-up around Turning, framing, and crawling back to space. and keep the pace playful.

## Main Activity Block
Play Escape the island game with soft partner pressure. and use short resets to reinforce Making space and moving into it quickly..

## Intro Grappling Block
Expose the students to Intro escape behavior without teaching a full pin escape system. with simple cues and lots of praise.

## Closing Script
Recap the main idea, reinforce the best example from the room, and set the expectation for live application.
