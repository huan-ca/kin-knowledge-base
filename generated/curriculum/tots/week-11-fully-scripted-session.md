# Tots Week 11 Fully Scripted Session

## Metadata
- Week: 11
- Cycle: partner
- Theme: Partner Connection And Positional Awareness
- Movement Theme: Staying connected while changing direction.
- Class Length: 30 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Partner Connection And Positional Awareness. Tell the class the goal is Partner Connection And Positional Awareness.

## Warm-Up Block
Run a movement-focused warm-up around Staying connected while changing direction. and keep the pace playful.

## Main Activity Block
Play Mirror partner game and follow-the-leader grappling patterns. and use short resets to reinforce Reaction, mirroring, and spatial awareness with a partner..

## Intro Grappling Block
Expose the students to Simple ideas of facing a partner, following motion, and staying safe. with simple cues and lots of praise.

## Closing Script
Recap the main idea, reinforce the best example from the room, and set the expectation for live application.
