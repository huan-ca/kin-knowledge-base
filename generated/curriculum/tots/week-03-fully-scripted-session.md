# Tots Week 03 Fully Scripted Session

## Metadata
- Week: 03
- Cycle: foundation
- Theme: Breakfalls And Rolling Confidence
- Movement Theme: Safe falling, rocking, and rolling.
- Class Length: 30 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Breakfalls And Rolling Confidence. Tell the class the goal is Breakfalls And Rolling Confidence.

## Warm-Up Block
Run a movement-focused warm-up around Safe falling, rocking, and rolling. and keep the pace playful.

## Main Activity Block
Play Crash-pad fall and recover challenge. and use short resets to reinforce Head safety, tucking, and recovering balance after falling..

## Intro Grappling Block
Expose the students to How to fall safely and get back to feet without panic. with simple cues and lots of praise.

## Closing Script
Recap the main idea, reinforce the best example from the room, and set the expectation for live application.
