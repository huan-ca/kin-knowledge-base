# Tots Week 04 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 04
- Cycle: foundation
- Theme: Bridging, Shrimping, And Turning
- Movement Theme: Hip lift, hip escape, and turning to a side.
- Class Length: 30 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Bridging, Shrimping, And Turning

## Coaching Emphasis
Short rounds and high reps work best here.

## Safety Notes
Keep rounds short, maintain high engagement, and prioritize movement quality over technical volume.

## Scaling Options
- Reduce complexity by shrinking the movement chain.
- Increase challenge by adding reaction and direction changes.
