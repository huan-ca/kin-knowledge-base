# Tots Week 04 Fully Scripted Session

## Metadata
- Week: 04
- Cycle: foundation
- Theme: Bridging, Shrimping, And Turning
- Movement Theme: Hip lift, hip escape, and turning to a side.
- Class Length: 30 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Bridging, Shrimping, And Turning. Tell the class the goal is Bridging, Shrimping, And Turning.

## Warm-Up Block
Run a movement-focused warm-up around Hip lift, hip escape, and turning to a side. and keep the pace playful.

## Main Activity Block
Play Bridge over the river and shrimp race. and use short resets to reinforce Hip control and moving the body sideways on the mat..

## Intro Grappling Block
Expose the students to Intro escape movement without formal pin pressure. with simple cues and lots of praise.

## Closing Script
Recap the main idea, reinforce the best example from the room, and set the expectation for live application.
