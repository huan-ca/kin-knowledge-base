# Tots Week 02 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 02
- Cycle: foundation
- Theme: Animal Movement And Locomotion
- Movement Theme: Bear crawls, crab walks, and low movement patterns.
- Class Length: 30 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Animal Movement And Locomotion

## Coaching Emphasis
Keep the language playful and use lots of imitation.

## Safety Notes
Keep rounds short, maintain high engagement, and prioritize movement quality over technical volume.

## Scaling Options
- Reduce complexity by shrinking the movement chain.
- Increase challenge by adding reaction and direction changes.
