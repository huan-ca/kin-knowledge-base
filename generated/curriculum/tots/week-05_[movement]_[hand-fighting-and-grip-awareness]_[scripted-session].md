# Tots Week 05 Fully Scripted Session

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Hand Fighting And Grip Awareness. Tell the class the goal is Hand Fighting And Grip Awareness.

## Warm-Up Block
Run a movement-focused warm-up around Touch, post, peel, and reconnect. and keep the pace playful.

## Main Activity Block
Play Hand-tag and grip-break reaction game. and use short resets to reinforce Fast hands and simple reaction timing..

## Intro Grappling Block
Expose the students to Hands in front, good posture, and gentle partner contact. with simple cues and lots of praise.

## Closing Script
Recap the main idea, reinforce the best example from the room, and set the expectation for live application.
