# Tots Week 02 Fully Scripted Session

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Animal Movement And Locomotion. Tell the class the goal is Animal Movement And Locomotion.

## Warm-Up Block
Run a movement-focused warm-up around Bear crawls, crab walks, and low movement patterns. and keep the pace playful.

## Main Activity Block
Play Animal race relays with short distances and resets. and use short resets to reinforce Cross-body movement and body awareness..

## Intro Grappling Block
Expose the students to Moving low to the floor and staying stable on hands and feet. with simple cues and lots of praise.

## Closing Script
Recap the main idea, reinforce the best example from the room, and set the expectation for live application.
