# Tots Week 09 Fully Scripted Session

## Metadata
- Week: 09
- Cycle: partner
- Theme: Top Pressure Through Movement Games
- Movement Theme: Staying heavy and connected on top without squeezing.
- Class Length: 30 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Top Pressure Through Movement Games. Tell the class the goal is Top Pressure Through Movement Games.

## Warm-Up Block
Run a movement-focused warm-up around Staying heavy and connected on top without squeezing. and keep the pace playful.

## Main Activity Block
Play Top turtle tag and chest-to-back balance game. and use short resets to reinforce Weight placement and following a moving partner..

## Intro Grappling Block
Expose the students to Simple top control ideas through playful contact. with simple cues and lots of praise.

## Closing Script
Recap the main idea, reinforce the best example from the room, and set the expectation for live application.
