## Coach Notes
Make passing about control of hips and chest, not just running around legs.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Headquarters posture, leg stapling, and knee-cut passing.
- Coach Notes: Students should identify when chest pressure replaces speed.

## Level 1
- Stabilize headquarters and complete one clean knee-cut to side control.

## Level 2
- Use a reaction from the defender to chain knee-cut into a second pass direction.
