## Coach Notes
The control phase matters more than rushing to the finish.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Side control pressure, far-side isolation, and americana mechanics.
- Coach Notes: Use shoulder pressure to pin rather than squeezing with the arms.

## Level 1
- Secure side control and finish the americana with clean elbow positioning.

## Level 2
- Flow from the americana threat into a straight-arm or mount transition when the partner defends.
