## Coach Notes
Students should recognize when posture is broken enough to attack without rushing the finish.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Closed guard armbar and triangle pathway with safe finishing mechanics.
- Coach Notes: Keep pressure directional and controlled; do not allow jumping into finishes.

## Level 1
- Attack the straight armbar or triangle after clearing posture.

## Level 2
- Chain armbar to triangle or back to posture-breaking if the first attack stalls.
