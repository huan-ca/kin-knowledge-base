# Youth Week 05 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 05
- Cycle: defensive
- Theme: Open Guard Retention And Knee-Elbow Recovery
- Teaching Goal: Show students how to keep their feet and knees in front of the passer and recover structure early.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Show students how to keep their feet and knees in front of the passer and recover structure early.

## Coaching Emphasis
Make the class feel like a retention game rather than a static technique block.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.
