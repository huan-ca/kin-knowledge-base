## Coach Notes
This week should feel athletic and reactive rather than overly technical.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Grip fighting, stance discipline, level-change awareness, and sprawls.
- Coach Notes: Keep the head above the hips and teach students to feel pressure changes.

## Level 1
- Win inside position and sprawl safely against a basic shot.

## Level 2
- Add a front-headlock or go-behind reaction after the sprawl succeeds.
