# Youth Week 22 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 22
- Cycle: defensive
- Theme: Choke Defense, Tap Awareness, And Reset Reactions
- Teaching Goal: Finish the defensive cycle by reinforcing hand fighting, neck safety, and calm reset behavior under submission pressure.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Finish the defensive cycle by reinforcing hand fighting, neck safety, and calm reset behavior under submission pressure.

## Coaching Emphasis
Keep the room composed; this week should build trust, not fear of submissions.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.
