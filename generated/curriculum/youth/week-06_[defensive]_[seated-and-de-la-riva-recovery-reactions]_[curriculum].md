## Snapshot
- Theme: Seated And De La Riva Recovery Reactions
- Cycle: defensive

## Coach Notes
Keep the focus on recovery and re-entry, not on building an advanced inversion game.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Recovering seated guard and De La Riva hooks after the passer clears the first line.
- Coach Notes: Teach students to win the inside space back before looking for offense.

## Level 1
- Rebuild frames and reconnect a seated or De La Riva entry.

## Level 2
- Use a second hook, collar grip, or angle change to off-balance the passer before recovering.
