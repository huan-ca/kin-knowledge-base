# Youth Week 11 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 11
- Cycle: offensive
- Theme: Side Control Control To Americana
- Teaching Goal: Teach top players how to secure side control before attacking one high-percentage youth-safe submission.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Teach top players how to secure side control before attacking one high-percentage youth-safe submission.

## Coaching Emphasis
The control phase matters more than rushing to the finish.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.
