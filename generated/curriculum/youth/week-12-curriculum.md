- Theme: Mount Progression To Armbar And Collar Attack
- Cycle: offensive
- Teaching Goal: Build mount progression from control into one clear attack pathway and one transition option.

## Coach Notes
Students should understand when to stay mounted and when to advance the attack.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Mount control, high mount progression, and armbar or collar-based attack entries.
- Coach Notes: Make knees and heels part of the control lesson, not an afterthought.

## Level 1
- Climb to a stable attacking mount and finish one clean armbar pathway.

## Level 2
- Switch between armbar and collar attack based on the defender’s hands.
