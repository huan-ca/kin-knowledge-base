## Coach Notes
Hand fighting and shoulder positioning should dominate the week.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Back defense, two-on-one hand fighting, and escaping to safe top or guard positions.
- Coach Notes: The first win is surviving the neck, not escaping fast.

## Level 1
- Clear the choking threat and escape one hook line.

## Level 2
- Chain the first escape into a turn or top-position recovery before the attacker resets.
