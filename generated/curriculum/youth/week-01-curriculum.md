## Coach Notes
Emphasize posture, breathing, and finding frames before explosive movement.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Early-stage escape structure from mount and side control.
- Coach Notes: Rule of three: frame, move hips, recover space.

## Level 1
- Bridge, frame, and recover half guard or closed guard.

## Level 2
- Chain the first bridge into a second direction change when the top player posts.
