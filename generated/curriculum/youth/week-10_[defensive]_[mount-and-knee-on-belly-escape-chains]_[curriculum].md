- Theme: Mount And Knee-On-Belly Escape Chains

## Coach Notes
Pressure should increase progressively so students feel realistic timing without panic.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Defensive reactions from mount and knee on belly into half guard or full guard recovery.
- Coach Notes: Keep the chin safe and make students solve the pressure with structure first.

## Level 1
- Bridge, frame, and recover a safe half guard.

## Level 2
- Chain bridge and elbow-knee reactions when the top player floats between mount and knee on belly.
