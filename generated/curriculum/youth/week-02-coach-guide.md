# Youth Week 02 Coach Guide

## Metadata
- Week: 02
- Cycle: defensive
- Theme: Late Escape Chains And Safe Guard Recovery
- Teaching Goal: Teach students how to recover when the first escape fails and pressure is already established.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Weekly Intent
Teach students how to recover when the first escape fails and pressure is already established.

## Coaching Emphasis
Use progressive resistance so students learn when to bridge, shrimp, and re-guard under pressure.

## Safety Notes
Match resistance to student readiness, reinforce tapping and partner care, and scale pace before complexity.

## Scaling Options
- Level 1 should focus on core mechanics and one clean finish or escape path.
- Level 2 should add chain options, timing, or more live reactions without changing the theme.
