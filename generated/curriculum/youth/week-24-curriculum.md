## Coach Notes
This week should feel like a review of the whole year through a self-defense lens.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Ground self-defense priorities: protect, frame, escape, stand, and create space.
- Coach Notes: Keep the final action practical: get safe and regain distance.

## Level 1
- Recover frames, escape a basic pin, and stand up safely.

## Level 2
- Add a second escape layer or controlled reversal before the stand-up when needed.
