## Coach Notes
Use progressive resistance so students learn when to bridge, shrimp, and re-guard under pressure.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Middle and late-stage escape chains from mount, knee on belly, and heavy side control.
- Coach Notes: Survival details stay small and always connect back to guard recovery.

## Level 1
- Use elbow-knee movement and hip recovery to rebuild guard.

## Level 2
- Add an ankle scoop or turn-in reaction when the top player overcommits.
