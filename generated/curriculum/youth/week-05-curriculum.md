## Coach Notes
Make the class feel like a retention game rather than a static technique block.

## Primary Focus
- Focus: Open guard retention, hip turns, and knee-elbow recovery against passing pressure.
- Coach Notes: Ecological framing works well here because students can react to real passing energy.

## Level 1
- Recover one knee line and rebuild a square guard.

## Level 2
- Add a second leg pummel or angle change to recover a stronger guard family.
