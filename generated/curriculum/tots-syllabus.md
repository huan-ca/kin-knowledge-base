# Tots Program Syllabus

This syllabus maps the tots weekly sequence to its cycle, theme, description, and main goal.

| Week | Cycle | Theme | Description | Main Goal |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 01 | foundation | Mat Rules, Base, And Ready Stance | Athletic base and stopping on command. | Make the first week feel structured, welcoming, and easy to win. |
| 02 | foundation | Animal Movement And Locomotion | Bear crawls, crab walks, and low movement patterns. | Keep the language playful and use lots of imitation. |
| 03 | foundation | Breakfalls And Rolling Confidence | Safe falling, rocking, and rolling. | Celebrate every safe fall; confidence matters more than polish. |
| 04 | foundation | Bridging, Shrimping, And Turning | Hip lift, hip escape, and turning to a side. | Short rounds and high reps work best here. |
| 05 | movement | Hand Fighting And Grip Awareness | Touch, post, peel, and reconnect. | Do not over-coach detail; keep this week active and reactive. |
| 06 | movement | Balance, Posture, And Push-Pull Games | Staying tall while resisting light pushes and pulls. | Use playful resistance and quick partner switches. |
| 07 | movement | Level Changes And Penetration Steps | Dropping levels and stepping forward without falling over. | Keep the movement smooth and rhythmic rather than technical. |
| 08 | movement | Stand-Up Base And Technical Stand-Up Play | Posting, standing safely, and recovering base. | Praise clean posts and safe eyes-forward recoveries. |
| 09 | partner | Top Pressure Through Movement Games | Staying heavy and connected on top without squeezing. | Use very short rounds so the room stays energetic. |
| 10 | partner | Escape Movement And Space-Making | Turning, framing, and crawling back to space. | This is still a fun movement week, not a technical pin week. |
| 11 | partner | Partner Connection And Positional Awareness | Staying connected while changing direction. | Use assistant coaches heavily to keep pairings productive. |
| 12 | review | Review Circuit And Fun Challenge Day | Mixing the favorite movements from the cycle into one class. | Finish the block with recognition, fun, and visible success for every child. |
