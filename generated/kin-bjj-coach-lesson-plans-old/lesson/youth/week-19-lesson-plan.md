# Week 19 (Defensive Cycle)
- Theme: Collar Tie to Knee Tap Fundamentals
- Teaching Goal: Build a connected collar tie to knee tap finish into passing off the finish into side control sequence.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Collar Tie to Knee Tap Fundamentals. Tell the class the goal is to connect the feet-to-floor entry with stable control on the ground.

## Warm-Up Options
- **Movement Based**:
  - Stance, level change, and movement-prep warm-up.
- **Drill Based**:
  - Partner entry reps with low resistance and quick resets.

## Lesson: Collar Tie to Knee Tap Fundamentals
- **Takedown**
  - Coaching Tip: Emphasize head position and connection through the tap.
  - Level 1: Collar tie to knee tap finish.
  - Level 2: Add timing, angle change, or chain-attack follow-up without increasing chaos.
  - Outcome: Finish to a clean top position that leads into passing off the finish into side control
- **Ground: Passing off the finish into side control**
  - Secure: Passing off the finish into side control.
  - Level 2: Add a simple transition or pressure-retention task before the finish.
- **Situational Options**
  - Start from the target control point and rotate on clear win conditions.
  - Run short rounds with a fast reset after the first clean win condition or escape.

## Coach Notes
- Youth framing: Use short cues, clear boundaries, and positive self-defence framing.
- Emphasize head position and connection through the tap.
- Keep the room moving with short explanation windows and clear reset points.

## Closing Script
- Recap the theme: Collar Tie to Knee Tap Fundamentals.
- Reinforce one safety cue and one successful habit from the room.
