# Adult Syllabus

- Program: Adult
- Total Weeks: 24
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Weekly Themes
- Week 01: Double Leg to Side Control Fundamentals - Build a connected double leg entry to a controlled finish on top into side control pin and basic top stabilization sequence.
- Week 02: Sprawl, Go-Behind, and Turtle Control Fundamentals - Build a connected sprawl reaction to go-behind finish into turtle control and seatbelt awareness sequence.
- Week 03: Single Leg to Knee-On-Belly Fundamentals - Build a connected single leg finish with strong posture into knee-on-belly stabilization and transitions sequence.
- Week 04: Body Lock Entry to Mount Fundamentals - Build a connected body lock entry with balance-breaking finish into mount retention and pressure sequence.
- Week 05: Snap Down to Front Headlock Fundamentals - Build a connected snap down into front headlock control into front headlock to spin-behind top control sequence.
- Week 06: Inside Trip to Half Guard Top Fundamentals - Build a connected inside trip with upper-body control into half guard top pressure and crossface discipline sequence.
- Week 07: Arm Drag to Rear Body Lock Fundamentals - Build a connected arm drag to rear angle or mat return into rear control entry and seatbelt management sequence.
- Week 08: Collar Tie to Knee Tap Fundamentals - Build a connected collar tie to knee tap finish into passing off the finish into side control sequence.
- Week 09: Duck Under to Side Control Fundamentals - Build a connected duck under entry to clean top position into side control switching and pin transitions sequence.
- Week 10: Ankle Pick to Mount Path Fundamentals - Build a connected ankle pick with posture break and angle into mount entry after a clean finish sequence.
- Week 11: Front Headlock to Go-Behind Fundamentals - Build a connected front headlock snap and spin finish into top control off the go-behind with chest pressure sequence.
- Week 12: Double Leg to Side Control Fundamentals - Build a connected double leg entry to a controlled finish on top into side control pin and basic top stabilization sequence.
- Week 13: Sprawl, Go-Behind, and Turtle Control Fundamentals - Build a connected sprawl reaction to go-behind finish into turtle control and seatbelt awareness sequence.
- Week 14: Single Leg to Knee-On-Belly Fundamentals - Build a connected single leg finish with strong posture into knee-on-belly stabilization and transitions sequence.
- Week 15: Body Lock Entry to Mount Fundamentals - Build a connected body lock entry with balance-breaking finish into mount retention and pressure sequence.
- Week 16: Snap Down to Front Headlock Fundamentals - Build a connected snap down into front headlock control into front headlock to spin-behind top control sequence.
- Week 17: Inside Trip to Half Guard Top Fundamentals - Build a connected inside trip with upper-body control into half guard top pressure and crossface discipline sequence.
- Week 18: Arm Drag to Rear Body Lock Fundamentals - Build a connected arm drag to rear angle or mat return into rear control entry and seatbelt management sequence.
- Week 19: Collar Tie to Knee Tap Fundamentals - Build a connected collar tie to knee tap finish into passing off the finish into side control sequence.
- Week 20: Duck Under to Side Control Fundamentals - Build a connected duck under entry to clean top position into side control switching and pin transitions sequence.
- Week 21: Ankle Pick to Mount Path Fundamentals - Build a connected ankle pick with posture break and angle into mount entry after a clean finish sequence.
- Week 22: Front Headlock to Go-Behind Fundamentals - Build a connected front headlock snap and spin finish into top control off the go-behind with chest pressure sequence.
- Week 23: Lower-Body Entry and Safety - Build a connected clinch-to-ashi entry with controlled descent into ashi garami positioning and partner safety sequence.
- Week 24: Lower-Body Defense and Exit - Build a connected entries that end in safe leg-entanglement awareness into boot line protection, hand fighting, and exit routes sequence.

## Coach Note
- Weeks 23-24 reserve lower-body focus with strong safety framing.
