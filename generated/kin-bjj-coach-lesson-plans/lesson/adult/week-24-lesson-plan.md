# Week 24 (Defensive Cycle)
- Theme: Lower-Body Defense and Exit
- Teaching Goal: Build a connected entries that end in safe leg-entanglement awareness into boot line protection, hand fighting, and exit routes sequence.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Lower-Body Defense and Exit. Tell the class the goal is to connect the feet-to-floor entry with stable control on the ground.

## Warm-Up Options
- **Movement Based**:
  - Stance, level change, and movement-prep warm-up.
- **Drill Based**:
  - Partner entry reps with low resistance and quick resets.

## Lesson: Lower-Body Defense and Exit
- **Takedown**
  - Coaching Tip: Keep this week defensive-first and tightly supervised.
  - Level 1: Entries that end in safe leg-entanglement awareness.
  - Level 2: Add timing, angle change, or chain-attack follow-up without increasing chaos.
  - Outcome: Finish to a clean top position that leads into boot line protection, hand fighting, and exit routes
- **Ground**
  - Secure: Boot line protection, hand fighting, and exit routes.
  - Level 2: Add a simple transition or pressure-retention task before the finish.
  - Submission / Win Condition: No new finish; focus on safe recognition and escape.

## Coach Notes
- Adult framing: Use adult pacing and decision-making language.
- Keep this week defensive-first and tightly supervised.
- Keep the room moving with short explanation windows and clear reset points.

## Situational Options
- Start from the target control point and rotate on clear win conditions.
- Run short rounds with a fast reset after the first clean win condition or escape.

## Closing Script
- Recap the theme: Lower-Body Defense and Exit.
- Reinforce one safety cue and one successful habit from the room.
