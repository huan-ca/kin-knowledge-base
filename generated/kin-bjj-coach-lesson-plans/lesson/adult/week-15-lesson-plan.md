# Week 15 (Defensive Cycle)
- Theme: Body Lock Entry to Mount Fundamentals
- Teaching Goal: Build a connected body lock entry with balance-breaking finish into mount retention and pressure sequence.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Body Lock Entry to Mount Fundamentals. Tell the class the goal is to connect the feet-to-floor entry with stable control on the ground.

## Warm-Up Options
- **Movement Based**:
  - Stance, level change, and movement-prep warm-up.
- **Drill Based**:
  - Partner entry reps with low resistance and quick resets.

## Lesson: Body Lock Entry to Mount Fundamentals
- **Takedown**
  - Coaching Tip: Teach pressure through chest connection before advancing.
  - Level 1: Body lock entry with balance-breaking finish.
  - Level 2: Add timing, angle change, or chain-attack follow-up without increasing chaos.
  - Outcome: Finish to a clean top position that leads into mount retention and pressure
- **Ground**
  - Secure: Mount retention and pressure.
  - Level 2: Add a simple transition or pressure-retention task before the finish.
  - Submission / Win Condition: Cross-collar or arm-isolation finish.

## Coach Notes
- Adult framing: Use adult pacing and decision-making language.
- Teach pressure through chest connection before advancing.
- Keep the room moving with short explanation windows and clear reset points.

## Situational Options
- Start from the target control point and rotate on clear win conditions.
- Run short rounds with a fast reset after the first clean win condition or escape.

## Closing Script
- Recap the theme: Body Lock Entry to Mount Fundamentals.
- Reinforce one safety cue and one successful habit from the room.
