# Week 10 (Offensive Cycle)
- Theme: Ankle Pick to Mount Path Fundamentals
- Teaching Goal: Build a connected ankle pick with posture break and angle into mount entry after a clean finish sequence.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Ankle Pick to Mount Path Fundamentals. Tell the class the goal is to connect the feet-to-floor entry with stable control on the ground.

## Warm-Up Options
- **Movement Based**:
  - Stance, level change, and movement-prep warm-up.
- **Drill Based**:
  - Partner entry reps with low resistance and quick resets.

## Lesson: Ankle Pick to Mount Path Fundamentals
- **Takedown**
  - Coaching Tip: Keep the pick low and the chest tall.
  - Level 1: Ankle pick with posture break and angle.
  - Level 2: Add timing, angle change, or chain-attack follow-up without increasing chaos.
  - Outcome: Finish to a clean top position that leads into mount entry after a clean finish
- **Ground**
  - Secure: Mount entry after a clean finish.
  - Level 2: Add a simple transition or pressure-retention task before the finish.
  - Submission / Win Condition: Mounted attack options and retention.

## Coach Notes
- Adult framing: Use adult pacing and decision-making language.
- Keep the pick low and the chest tall.
- Keep the room moving with short explanation windows and clear reset points.

## Situational Options
- Start from the target control point and rotate on clear win conditions.
- Run short rounds with a fast reset after the first clean win condition or escape.

## Closing Script
- Recap the theme: Ankle Pick to Mount Path Fundamentals.
- Reinforce one safety cue and one successful habit from the room.
