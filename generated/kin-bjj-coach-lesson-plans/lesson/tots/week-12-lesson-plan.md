# Week 12 (Review)
Theme: Review and Celebration
Teaching Goal: Build fun, attention, athletic movement, and comfort on the mat.
Class Length: 30 minutes

## Lesson
### Introduce the Theme
- Tell the class the goal is Review and Celebration.
- Coaching Emphasis: A coach should think about timing, transitions, attention span, and behavior management as part of lesson quality.
- Coaching Emphasis: A systematic class structure also makes it easier to deliver the same standard across different instructors.

### Warm-Up Block
- Favorite movement review
- Coaching Emphasis: The adult and youth examples group warm-up ideas into movement-based, drill-based, and CLA-based options instead of a single required script.
- Coaching Emphasis: The adult and youth movement options include duck walks, breakfalls, and bear crawls with backsteps.

### Main Activity Block
- Coach-choice game circuit
- Keep the game moving quickly and stop before attention drops.

### Intro Grappling Block
- Best habits from the full cycle
- Keep contact gentle and reset early when posture breaks.

### Coach Notes
- Keep the pace playful, the instructions short, and the praise immediate.
- Keep transitions short and celebrate good listening quickly.
- Coaching Emphasis: Coaches should not evaluate class quality only by how many techniques were shown. Room culture, effort, attention, and behavior are part of the program’s success criteria.

### Closing Script
- Recap the theme, praise the best example from the room, and finish with a clear dismissal.
