# Week 7 (Movement)
Theme: Change of Direction
Teaching Goal: Build fun, attention, athletic movement, and comfort on the mat.
Class Length: 30 minutes

## Lesson
### Introduce the Theme
- Tell the class the goal is Change of Direction.

### Warm-Up Block
- Shuffle, pivot, and drop steps

### Main Activity Block
- Corner chase game

### Intro Grappling Block
- Turning hips without falling

### Coach Notes
- Keep the pace playful, the instructions short, and the praise immediate.
- Keep transitions short and celebrate good listening quickly.

### Closing Script
- Recap the theme, praise the best example from the room, and finish with a clear dismissal.
