# Week 20 (Defensive Cycle)
- Theme: Duck Under to Side Control Fundamentals
- Teaching Goal: Build a connected duck under entry to clean top position into side control switching and pin transitions sequence.
- Class Length: 60 minutes

## Opening Script
Introduce the theme: Duck Under to Side Control Fundamentals. Tell the class the goal is to connect the feet-to-floor entry with stable control on the ground.

## Warm-Up Options
- **Movement Based**:
  - Stance, level change, and movement-prep warm-up.
- **Drill Based**:
  - Partner entry reps with low resistance and quick resets.

## Lesson: Duck Under to Side Control Fundamentals
- **Takedown**
  - Coaching Tip: Use timing and posture, not arm strength.
  - Level 1: Duck under entry to clean top position.
  - Level 2: Add timing, angle change, or chain-attack follow-up without increasing chaos.
  - Outcome: Finish to a clean top position that leads into side control switching and pin transitions
- **Ground: Side control switching and pin transitions**
  - Secure: Side control switching and pin transitions.
  - Level 2: Add a simple transition or pressure-retention task before the finish.
- **Situational Options**
  - Start from the target control point and rotate on clear win conditions.
  - Run short rounds with a fast reset after the first clean win condition or escape.

## Coach Notes
- Youth framing: Use short cues, clear boundaries, and positive self-defence framing.
- Use timing and posture, not arm strength.
- Keep the room moving with short explanation windows and clear reset points.

## Closing Script
- Recap the theme: Duck Under to Side Control Fundamentals.
- Reinforce one safety cue and one successful habit from the room.
